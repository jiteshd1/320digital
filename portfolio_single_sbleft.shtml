Project: Checkout development for in-store touch-screen Kiosks As part of Tesco’s Multichannel strategy the requirement was to develop and integrate a full checkout journey on 330 touch-screen Kiosk devices allowing customers to purchase Tesco Direct products unattended in-stores. I also led the integration of a chip and pin, barcode scanner and receipt printing devices. The proof of concept is now production ready to be launched I October. A significant amount of team facilitation, coaching and influencing stakeholders at Director Level was required to deliver this requirement.
